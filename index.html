FlyBySky: Social Travel

Mobile Travel App Case Study

The goal of this project was to design a mobile website interface that would allow users to easily book tickets to the destinations they see on social media. This project's scope included conducting user research using phone interviews, organizing the data into affinity diagrams and empathy maps to develop a user persona, then using the insights gleaned to create a problem statement. Then, after brainstorming and ideating, a value proposition was created to guide the design process. At the high level, a storyboard was created to understand the user's journey and motivations. Finally, a digital wireframe was built and presented to users for further testing, and the feedback was used to iteratively improve the design into a Lo-Fi Prototype, and ultimately, a High Fidelity Prototype of FlyBySky's introduction screens and flight search and booking process.

Gathering information from users about their feelings, motivations, and routines is vital to creating a design that will present relevant information in a format that is easy to consume. Four users of varying age and ethnographic backgrounds were interviewed regarding various aspects of their travel planning such as frequency, mode of travel, and safety precautions. Various competitors in the travel app space that users were already familiar with were also analyzed to find which important features were necessary as well as which could be improved. The key takeaways from the interviews were that travelers planned activities first before selecting a destination, were very concerned about cleanliness and sanitation practices, took long trips mostly for sightseeing and to visit friends, and booked trips around 2 months ahead of time. From the competitor analysis, it was noted that travel apps took steps to reassure users that safety practices were being put in place and provided information regarding quarantine requirements.

Based on the User Research, the insight was gained that a young, well-educated and curious traveler wants to maximize the enjoyment of their vacation with activities that suit their interests.
